Vocal Warmups

Prepare your voice for performance.

Warmup Routine

  • 1. Breathing Exercises
    Breathe in for 4 counts, hold for 4, exhale for 8. Repeat 5 times. Focus on expanding your diaphragm.
  • 2. Lip Trills
    Relax your lips and blow air through them to create a "brrr" sound. Slide up and down your range for 2 minutes.
  • 3. Humming
    Hum gently on "mmm" through your comfortable range. Feel the vibration in your mask (face). 2 minutes.
  • 4. Sirens
    Using "oo" or "ee", slide from your lowest comfortable note to your highest and back down. Repeat 5-10 times.
  • 5. Scales on Vowels
    Sing 5-note scales (do-re-mi-fa-sol-fa-mi-re-do) on each vowel: Ah, Eh, Ee, Oh, Oo. Move up by half steps.
  • 6. Tongue Twisters
    "Red leather yellow leather" • "Unique New York" • "The lips, the teeth, the tip of the tongue" - start slow, increase speed.
  • 7. Resonance Work
    Speak or sing "mee-may-mah-moh-moo" focusing on forward placement. Feel the sound resonate in your cheekbones.
  • 8. Song Excerpt
    Sing through an easy, comfortable song from your repertoire at 50% volume to bring everything together.
